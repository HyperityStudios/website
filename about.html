About Us

Our journey started in early 2018 when a bunch of gamers got together with one simple goal: helping others navigate their favourite games. As we worked together, we realised we loved creating games even more. So, on the 11th of January, 2022, Hyperity was officially born as a game development studio, and we set out on a mission to craft unforgettable gaming experiences.

At Hyperity, we believe that collaboration between developers and players is key. We’re all about using player feedback to make our games better, building a community of passionate gamers who love gaming as much as we do. Our team is a mix of developers, artists, and writers from different backgrounds and cultures, all working together to create games that captivate and inspire.
